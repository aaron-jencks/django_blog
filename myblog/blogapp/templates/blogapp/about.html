Bio:

Hi, I am an enthusiast in writing code, I feel like I should share my experiences with the world. I often learn about interesting things, most of the time technology related, sometimes not, while I'm working on projects. Some of them are really obscure and I feel like these things should be shown to the world.

Background:

I am a student at the Iowa State University (Go Cyclones!), I've been writing code since I was in high school. I started writing batch in my sophomore year, creating console animations, etc... Shortly after I discovered python and began working with that, though it was short-lived. Once I got into college at Kirkwood Community College I learned C, C++, and LabVIEW. I also taught myself c#, around this time I got into machine learning, and I dove straight into it.
I'm still learning about machine learning, and hope to learn a lot as this website grows. When I first began learning about AI and ML, I created a toolkit in c#, thus I have little experience with python or R, or the other common machine learning languages.
But all that's about to change...
